Aquapulse

Aquapulse represents a state-of-the-art smart irrigation system intricately designed to elevate agricultural water efficiency. Utilizing the capabilities of Arduino and NodeMCU microcontrollers, it skillfully collects and organizes precise data from Soil Moisture Sensors. This information is seamlessly transmitted wirelessly to the backend Flask Server. Enhancing user interaction, Aquapulse features an engaging User Portal, providing not just a visualization of operations but also placing control of the entire process at users' fingertips. This groundbreaking innovation seamlessly integrates technology and agriculture, paving the way for a sustainable and optimized future.

Tech Utilised: Arduino Uno, NodeMCU, Flask, React

VAST Challenge 2022: Patterns of Life

In the VAST Mini Challenge 2022 titled "Patterns of Life," the endeavor utilized visual analysis techniques to illuminate the everyday life of the Ohio city. The primary aim was to simplify the city’s complex patterns, such as daily routines, traffic flow, and overarching urban trends, using a variety of visualization tools. A profound insight into the diverse neighborhoods of the city was gained by creating maps with color-coded buildings that indicated their usage and rental prices. Traffic congestion throughout the week and on weekends was visualized with animated lines on the maps, pinpointing critical traffic hotspots. Such data is invaluable for urban developers, providing guidance on where to focus infrastructural enhancements. Additionally, the patterns of city dwellers' daily lives were captured and visualized through careful analysis of pertinent data.

The creation of these unique visualizations was achieved using D3.js and involved processing over 18GB of raw data. The UI was meticulously designed using Bootstrap and the latest Materials UI Design, ensuring a seamless and aesthetically pleasing user experience. Seven distinct visualizations were successfully amalgamated into a unified User Interface that could be easily managed through a central Control Panel.

Tech Utilised: Javascript, D3.js, Charts.js, Bootstrap, AJAX, Materials UI

Snap Split

Enter Snap Split—an innovative mobile application revolutionizing number forecasting through the power of deep learning. Designed for Android devices, this cutting-edge app goes beyond image recognition—it predicts numbers extracted from images with unparalleled accuracy. Snap Split doesn't just identify numbers; it organizes them into folders, streamlining your digital experience.

What sets Snap Split apart is its strategic use of parallel processing. Embracing a collaborative approach, it divides images into four segments, dispatching each to distinct servers for decentralized number prediction. The results are then harmonized, ensuring heightened precision. Finally, Snap Split intelligently archives images into appropriate sub-folders based on the conclusive predictions. Embrace the future of numerical foresight with Snap Split.

Tech Utilised: PyTorch, Android Studio, Java, Flask

SportZify

Introducing the 'SportZify' project, a revolutionary Xamarin-based, cross-platform mobile application designed to simplify sports complex and gym time slot bookings. Embracing the MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) architecture, this project ensures a seamless and engaging user experience. 'SportZify' marks a leap in innovation within cross-platform mobile app development, showcasing a shared codebase for both iOS and Android versions.

SportZify encompasses diverse pages, such as CricketPage.xaml, BadmintonPage.xaml, and GymPage.xaml, offering insights into sports complexes. Detailed views like SportZifyCricketDetail.xaml and SportZifyGymDetail.xaml provide in-depth information. The app features a dedicated slot booking view, named BookSlot.xaml. Three pivotal models—BadmintonCourt.cs, CricketClub.cs, and GymPlace.cs—establish getters and setters, streamlining interaction with the SQLite Database. This lightweight localized database seamlessly syncs with the central master database, ensuring efficient data management and retrieval.

Tech Utilised: Xamarin, C#, XAML, Canva, SQLite, Xcode, Android Emulator
